This credential certifies that the bearer has accomplished the following:

  • 1. Understand the nature of language.
  • 2. Understand various conceptions of language acquisition.
  • 3. Understand historical and current approaches to language teaching.
  • 4. Understand the importance of knowing about the structure of the English language and be equipped to pursue ongoing language development.
  • 5. Be able to use appropriate strategies and select appropriate teaching techniques for different English learning contexts.
  • 6. Be able to prepare an effective lesson plan for a specific English learning context.
  • 7. Be knowledgeable about the field of TESOL and understand important terms and acronyms in this field.
